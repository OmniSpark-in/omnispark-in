How we build

Design principles
we never
compromise on

Every decision at Omnispark is filtered through a simple question: is this good for the child? These are the principles we hold as non-negotiable.


Read our story →
01

Child safety is a hard constraint, not a feature

We don't serve ads to children, we don't collect unnecessary data, and we don't use dark patterns. Full stop. These aren't optional settings.

02

Offline-first for real-world India

Internet connectivity is inconsistent across India. Our apps are designed to work fully without a connection, so no child is left behind because of bandwidth.

03

Parents are co-designers

Every app we build includes parent visibility tools. Learning apps should bring families closer to their children's progress, not replace that relationship.

04

Joy is not the enemy of rigor

We believe learning can be genuinely delightful AND educationally sound. The best kid apps prove you don't have to choose between engaging and effective.

05

Small team, deep focus

We're deliberately small. That means fewer apps, built with more care — not a factory of mediocre products chasing download numbers.
