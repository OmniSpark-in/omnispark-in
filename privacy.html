1. About GyanQuest

GyanQuest is a free educational app for children in Classes 1–5 (typically ages 6–11). It is developed and published by Omnispark, an independent studio based in India. The app provides practice questions, progress tracking, and Olympiad preparation across Mathematics, Science, General Knowledge, and English.

GyanQuest is designed to work offline, meaning most features do not require an internet connection. The app contains no advertisements and is completely free to use.

2. Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • All users of the GyanQuest Android app
  • All visitors to omnispark.in
  • Parents and guardians who create accounts on behalf of their children

Because GyanQuest is designed for children, we require a parent or guardian to create and manage any account. Children do not interact directly with account registration or data settings.

3. What information we collect

Information you provide

  • Parent or guardian email address — used to create and manage your account and receive account-related communications
  • Child's first name and class / grade — used to personalise the learning experience and display progress in the parent dashboard

We do not collect a child's surname, date of birth, phone number, photograph, or physical address.

Information collected automatically

  • App usage data — topics practised, questions answered, scores, and time spent. This is stored locally on the device by default and only synced to our servers if you enable cloud backup.
  • Crash and diagnostic data — device type, OS version, and app version, collected anonymously to help us fix bugs.
  • Anonymous session identifiers — if you use cloud sync, we generate a random ID to associate your progress data with your account. This ID is not linked to any external identity.

We do NOT collect:

  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Contacts, call logs, or SMS data
  • Payment or financial information (the app is free)
  • Social media profiles or login credentials from other services
  • Behavioural data used for advertising or profiling

4. How we use your information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • To display your child's learning progress in the parent dashboard
  • To personalise practice sessions based on the child's class and past performance
  • To diagnose and fix app crashes and technical issues
  • To respond to support or privacy requests from parents and guardians
  • To send important account-related emails (such as password reset). We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.
We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build advertising profiles, sell user data, or share data with ad networks. GyanQuest will never show personalised ads to children.

5. Children's privacy (under 13)

GyanQuest is designed for children and we apply the following protections as required by the Google Play Families Policy and applicable child privacy laws:

  • Parental account creation: A parent or guardian must create the account. Children do not go through any registration flow themselves.
  • No personal data from children without parental consent: We do not knowingly collect any personal data from a child unless a parent or guardian has provided it during account setup.
  • No behavioural advertising: GyanQuest does not display interest-based or behavioural advertisements to any user, including children.
  • No social features: Children cannot post publicly, message other users, or share personal information within the app.
  • No in-app purchases: The app is entirely free. There are no purchases, subscriptions, or virtual currency systems.
  • Parental controls: Parents can review all data stored about their child and request its deletion at any time (see Section 8).

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that data promptly. Please contact us immediately if you believe this has occurred.

6. Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate GyanQuest. Each has been selected for its compliance with children's privacy standards:

  • Google Firebase (authentication and crash analytics) — Firebase processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policies. Crash reports sent to Firebase are anonymised and do not contain personal information about children.
  • Google Play Services — used for app distribution and core Android functionality. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, social media SDKs, or analytics tools that build user profiles or track users across apps.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data shared with the service providers above is strictly for operating the app. They are contractually prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

7. Data storage and retention

  • Local storage: Quiz progress and scores are stored on your device by default. Uninstalling the app removes this data.
  • Cloud storage: If you enable cloud sync, data is stored on secure servers provided by Google Firebase. Firebase infrastructure meets international security standards.
  • Retention period: We retain account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.
  • Crash reports: Anonymised crash logs are retained for up to 90 days and then automatically deleted.

8. Your rights as a parent or guardian

As the parent or guardian of a child using GyanQuest, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about your child.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your child's account and all associated personal data. We will complete this within 30 days.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time. This may affect the app's functionality (such as the parent dashboard).
  • Grievance: Lodge a complaint with us if you believe your data rights have not been respected (see Section 9 for our Grievance Officer details).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address in Section 12. We do not charge a fee for these requests and will respond within 30 days.

9. Account and Data Deletion

If you want to permanently delete your GyanQuest account and personal data held by Omnispark, you can do that here. This section explains how to submit a deletion request by email, without needing to reinstall, open, or log into the GyanQuest app.

You do not need to reinstall, open, or log into the GyanQuest app to complete this request. Simply send your request by email and our team will handle the deletion process promptly.

Request data deletion via email

What will be deleted

  • Parent or guardian account details and registered email
  • Child profile information entered in GyanQuest
  • App preferences, learning progress, and cloud backup data
  • Any account-linked project or usage data stored on our servers
  • Minimal audit or compliance records required by law
  • Anonymous crash and diagnostic logs retained for up to 90 days
  • Metadata needed to prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents

If you have questions about the deletion process for GyanQuest, contact us at privacy@omnispark.in.

10. Legal Compliance

Omnispark operates in compliance with applicable data privacy laws regulations. We are committed to protecting our users' data rights globally and adhere to the following core data protection principles:

  • Purpose Limitation: We only collect and process personal data that is strictly necessary for the operations and features described explicitly in this policy.
  • Parental Consent: We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without obtaining verifiable parental or guardian consent where required by regional law.
  • Safe Processing: We do not engage in any data processing tracking behaviors that could harm a child’s well-being, including behavioral monitoring, targeting, or commercial profiling.
  • Data Accuracy: We take all reasonable steps to ensure that the personal data we maintain is kept accurate, complete, and up to date.

Privacy Compliance Contact

11. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the personal data we hold, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS / TLS for all network communication
  • Access controls so that only authorised personnel can access personal data
  • Use of Firebase Authentication to securely manage user credentials — we never store raw passwords
  • Regular review of our data practices and security measures

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your account or your child's data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@omnispark.in.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our app evolves or as legal requirements change. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • For significant changes, notify users via an in-app message or email to the registered parent / guardian address

We encourage parents and guardians to review this policy periodically. Continued use of GyanQuest after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
